Experiment Aim

To study and verify Fourier's Law of Heat Conduction by determining the thermal conductivity of a given material using a 1D steady-state heat flow simulation.

Learning Objectives:

  • Understand the relationship between heat transfer rate, area, and temperature gradient.
  • Identify the proportionality constant (k) known as thermal conductivity.
  • Observe how different materials (conductors vs. insulators) affect the rate of heat flow.
  • Apply the Fourier Law equation: Q = -kA(dT/dx) in a virtual laboratory environment.
